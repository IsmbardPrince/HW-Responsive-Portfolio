About Me

I have navigated a major portion of my life on a road of ideas. Those pursuits that I have excelled at and which have given me the most pleasure and personal gain are those whose physical artifacts are not really the products but actually just representations of very real but non-material products. It is why I find myself back again searching for interesting new problems to solve through software based technologies.


The experience I have accumulated in software related activities over the years still provides a solid basis for analysis and evaluation in today's rapidly shifting technology programs, but the new paradigms in design, deployment, support and other areas stimulate new ways to apply that experience. I personally find it invigorating to meet the challenge of sifting through the multitude of options available to create functionality or solve problems and select the optimal combination for achieving it. And it is definitely a good reason to get up in the morning.
